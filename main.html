Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 1, 2025

1. Introduction

Equipr ("we," "our," or "the App") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our iOS application.

2. Information We Collect

We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information.

Specifically:

  • No account creation or login required
  • No personal information collected
  • No usage analytics or tracking
  • No third-party advertising or data sharing

3. Camera Usage

Equipr requires camera access to identify gym equipment. However:

  • Camera images are processed entirely on your device using CoreML
  • No photos or camera data are saved, uploaded, or transmitted
  • No images leave your device
  • Processing happens in real-time and images are discarded immediately after analysis

4. Data Storage

The only data stored on your device includes:

  • Your saved workout plans (stored locally using UserDefaults)
  • App preferences and settings
  • Onboarding completion status

All data remains on your device and is never transmitted to our servers or any third parties.

5. Third-Party Services

Equipr may link to external YouTube videos for exercise demonstrations. When you tap to view a video:

  • You will be directed to YouTube (either the app or website)
  • YouTube's privacy policy and terms of service will apply
  • We do not control or have access to any data collected by YouTube

6. Children's Privacy

Equipr does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children under the age of 13. The App does not require or request any personal information from users of any age.

7. Data Security

Since all data processing happens on your device and no data is transmitted or stored on external servers, your information remains secure on your iOS device, protected by your device's built-in security features.

8. Your Rights

You have complete control over your data:

  • Delete all app data by deleting the app from your device
  • Clear workout plans through the app's settings
  • Revoke camera permissions through iOS Settings at any time

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I use the app?

Simply open the app, allow camera access, and point your camera at any gym machine. The AI will automatically detect and identify the equipment.

Why isn't my camera working?

Make sure you've granted camera permissions. Go to Settings > Privacy & Security > Camera > Equipr and enable access.

Can I use the app offline?

Yes! All AI processing happens on your device. You only need internet to watch YouTube video demonstrations.

How do I add exercises to my workout plan?

After detecting a machine, tap the detection card, then tap "Add to My Workout" and select your training days.

How do I delete my data?

You can clear all workout plans through the debug menu (ladybug icon) or by deleting and reinstalling the app.

Is my data private?

Absolutely! All data stays on your device. We never collect, store, or transmit your personal information.
